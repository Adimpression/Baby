Many ask us to explain this website in one line. This is that:

"If you believe that living life happy
is about meeting up with friends and family to have a great time,
this website helps you do that."


That, it does best because it was built with that core belief.
Happy Socializing!


What we are not:


It is clearly not a social network and does not have unrelated stuff like the "like" button.
It just helps you hangout with people you care.


What would you first need,

>to meet up with friends or family? An appropriate place
>to find an appropriate place? Good recommendations
