Album
-----

Album should have owners and visitors
Owners to manage it
Visitors to limit access


Albums should be many to many
Many humans may point to one album
One human may point to many albums


Albums can be viewed regardless of location or event view/own policies


Location Private/Public
-----------------------

Locations Private - Public differentiate only in the sense that who can see it.
=> For users, they still will be made to known as "Locations" with a sense of "My Locations".

Events Public/Private
---------------------

Events Public - Private differentiate only in the sense that owners/viewers.
=> An owner/owners makes it. Viewers are "everybody" without restriction.
