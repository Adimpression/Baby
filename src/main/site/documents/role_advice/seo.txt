The intention of this document is to provide some guidelines to an SEO specialist.

1. When using keywords, try to put them in present tense. e.g Travel to India => Traveling to India

This approach grabs both Search engine terms Travel + Traveling.
This approach gives the user more faith in what is being told, and a sense of togetherness.
